Describe your app in 2-4 short paragraphs focused on user value.

Example:
YourApp helps people capture information, organize tasks, and stay on top of daily work from one simple interface.

Use this description to explain what problem the app solves, who it is for, and what makes it useful. Keep the language specific and practical instead of generic marketing filler.

Suggested structure:
- Paragraph 1: what the app does
- Paragraph 2: the core workflows or standout features
- Paragraph 3: who benefits from it and why
